V I N Y L

Vinyl was a web app prototype developed for an existing bar/restaurant. The task was to improve customer engagement by providing table ordering via digital devices. Customers are able to use touch screens at their table or booth and tablet devices mounted at the bar to order food and drinks. They required a system that enables customers to easily order and see the progress of their order. This was my first ever solo website made. It was a huge learning experience for me in the world of HTML and CSS
